About me

I am a dedicated full-stack developer with expertise in both frontend and backend technologies, creating responsive, user-centric digital experiences optimized for performance across all devices. I’m proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and EJS, with additional experience in Flutter and Dart for cross-platform mobile apps. My keen eye for clean design and seamless interactions drives me to build scalable web applications that delight users.

My portfolio highlights a range of hands-on projects: a fully responsive personal website; Elephanta Quiz, a dynamic quiz platform with personalized user management; VLC Media Player Web Clone with modern playback controls; a MERN-stack Airbnb replica with authentication, image uploads, and location-based search; and CareerCraft, an AI-driven career development platform powered by Google Gemini AI for personalized advice, mock interviews, and resume building. I also interned at Prodigy Infotech, where I developed and optimized interactive web apps (landing pages, a stopwatch, Tic-Tac-Toe, and a weather app) and earned 2nd prize in the SOUIVTHON hackathon for an AI-driven online proctoring solution.

I hold a Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) from Silver Oak University, graduating with a 9.30 CGPA. I’m continually expanding my toolkit—currently learning React and exploring advanced backend architectures—while actively engaging in workshops and hackathons to stay at the forefront of industry trends. With strong communication, collaboration, and UI/UX design skills, I’m eager to contribute to impactful projects in a dynamic, team-oriented environment.
